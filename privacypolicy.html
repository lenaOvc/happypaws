Privacy Policy

Happy Paws is managed by Dublinners and Corkeans Ltd. This policy regulates all the activities performed by us. Keeping your data secure is our priority. All our activities comply with the Data Protection Acts 1998 and 2003 and with the General Data Protection Regulation. When you provide us with your personal data, (such as the name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number) you agree to its transfer, storing or processing as we see necessary and in line with data protection legislation and guidelines to make possible the provision of our services to you. We are committed to ensure that your data is managed and kept securely following the guidelines of this Privacy Policy. We are transparent in our activities and any changes we apply to our Privacy Policy, to the way we collect, process and store information will be made available through our Happy Paws web site.

Personal Information: What We Collect and Why?

When you share with us your personal information, either by telephone, in-store or online, we only ask you for the most relevant minimum information we need to allow us to provide you with a full service. This includes your name and email address. Additional contact details or additional information may be requested to provide a complete service, or to ensure we can reach you in the best and most preferred by you way to complete the provision of our service, or to ensure an effective, efficient, clear and quick communication regarding any events organized or updates that you expressed your wish to receive. We may need to contact you by phone in order to confirm a service or answer a question.

Your Rights

The European law grants specific rights to the individuals like you:

1. The right of confirmation. You can ask for clear details and confirmation on how we process your data, and request to apply changes to the way we process your data.
2. The right to access data. You can ask for information on how we store your personal data and a copy of these details.
3. The right to rectification. You can ask to rectify or correct the data we have about you.
4. The right to be forgotten. You can ask us to erase your data completely from our systems.
5. The right to restrict processing of data. You can restrict the way we process your data.
6. The right to object processing of data. You can ask us to stop processing your data.
7. The right to data portability. You can ask us to transmit your personal data directly from our organisation to another.
8. The right to refuse automated individual decision-making, including profiling.
9. The right to withdraw data protection consent. You can change your mind at any time if you wish.

The data controller for the purposes of GDPR is Dublinners and Corkeans Limited. Happy Paws Limited is the data processor for the company. You can contact our Data Protection Officer at DataProtection@happypaws.ie if you have any additional questions on data protection and privacy policy.

Non-Personal Information

We also collect statistical and analytical data of all our customers that visit our website and our grooming facilities. We only need this data for improving the quality of the service we provide to you. This information is kept separately from your personal details and is completely anonymous.
