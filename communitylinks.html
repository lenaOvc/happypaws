Community Links

Through our Happy Paws site, we are striving to provide only useful and interesting information. We are taking every reasonable step in our efforts to present this information accurately and up to date. The data related to the third-parties, however, may be changing faster than can be predicted, therefore, we cannot fully guarantee that it is accurate, relevant, complete and timeliness at the time of your search. If you observe any mistake or omission, please reach out to us directly by email info@happypaws.com and we will be happy to correct it as soon as possible.
In addition, the order in which the hyperlinks are presented does not attempt to show their importance and does not pursue any marketing interests. The hyperlinks are organized in an alphabetical order. Please reach out to us if you observe that any of these links unknowingly and unintentionally lead to inappropriate websites. We will take the necessary actions to correct this with the highest priority.
